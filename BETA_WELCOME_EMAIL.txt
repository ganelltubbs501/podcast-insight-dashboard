## What LoquiHQ Does

LoquiHQ analyzes your podcast transcripts using Gemini AI to extract:

- **Monetization Opportunities**: Sponsorship recommendations and revenue projections
- **Content Strategy**: Repurposing suggestions for social media, email, and more
